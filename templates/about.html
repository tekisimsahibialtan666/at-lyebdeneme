About Us

Atölye B Mimarlık is an architecture and interior design practice based in Ankara. We approach every commission with curiosity and care, shaping environments that are deeply rooted in their context and crafted with refined detail.

Our Practice

We believe in architecture as a collaborative journey. Each project is a dialogue between client, site, and community. Our studio delivers integrated solutions that encompass architectural design, interior experiences, and urban interventions. We embrace sustainable practices, advanced technologies, and local craftsmanship to deliver enduring value.

Mission

To create thoughtful spaces that enhance the quality of life, celebrate cultural narratives, and respect environmental resources.

Vision

To be recognized for innovative, contextually responsive designs that connect people to their surroundings and to one another.
